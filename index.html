Environmental issues are effects of human activity on the biophysical environment, most often of which are harmful effects that cause environmental degradation.[citation needed][dubious – discuss] Environmental protection is a practice of protecting the natural environment on the individual, organizational or governmental levels, for the benefit of both the environment and humans. Environmentalism, a social and environmental movement, addresses environmental issues through advocacy, legislation education and activism.
